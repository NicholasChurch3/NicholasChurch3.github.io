My Journey into Data Analysis:

In 2021, an article sparked my curiosity about data analysis. The idea of leveraging data to solve problems and improve efficiency resonated with me, leading me to explore this exciting field. I began by taking the Google Data Analytics course on Coursera, and as my interest grew, I actively pursued additional learning resources.

My background is diverse, encompassing experiences in construction, engineering, system administration and Information Technology. This has given me a valuable perspective on how businesses operate and what customers truly value. I believe this broad lens allows me to better understand stakeholders' needs and translate data into actionable insights.

Currently, I'm a Business Intelligence Analyst eager to contribute my skills and knowledge to the field. I'm passionate about using data to uncover meaningful insights, optimize processes, and ultimately, help businesses thrive.
